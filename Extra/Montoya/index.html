Making A Difference

Montoya Custom Homes is a family-owned business operated by Baltazar Montoya. Baltazar is committed to building homes for families searching for a quality custom home and a personal builder who is dedicated to the task.

Montoya Custom Homes has grown their business by building quality custom homes in many of Amarillo's neighborhoods. Baltazar takes pride in his attention to detail from the planning stages through construction and beyond. Montoya Custom Homes builds on a solid foundation of integrity, honesty, knowledge, and accountability. Baltazar strives to form lasting positive relationships with his clients while maintaining an excellent reputation in the community.
