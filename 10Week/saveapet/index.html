Making A Difference

At Save-A-Pet we strive to provide good animals with good homes. We care and tend for our animals as if they are family.

Whenever you're ready to adopt we're here to help. Our shelter is a no kill shelter and this is all thanks to our donors, foster families, and of course, our adopters.
